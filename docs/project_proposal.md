## Project Title

PetConnect: A Web Application for Pet Adoption and Rehoming

## Introduction

PetConnect is a web application designed to help connect pet owners, shelters, and individuals who are looking to adopt pets. Many pets need new homes due to changes in their owners' circumstances, while many families are searching for pets to adopt. Existing platforms are often scattered across social media groups or classified websites, making it difficult to find reliable and organized information.

The goal of PetConnect is to provide a centralized platform where users can post pets available for adoption or rehoming, browse available pets, and communicate with pet owners through a simple and user-friendly interface.

## Problem Statement

People looking to adopt pets often struggle to find trustworthy and up-to-date information. Likewise, pet owners who need to rehome their pets may not have an effective platform to reach responsible adopters. Current solutions are fragmented across multiple websites and social media platforms, making the adoption process inefficient.

A dedicated web application can simplify this process by organizing pet listings, improving search capabilities, and providing a reliable communication channel between pet owners and potential adopters.

## Project Objectives

The objectives of PetConnect are to:

- Provide a centralized platform for pet adoption and rehoming.
- Allow users to create and manage pet listings.
- Enable users to search and filter pets based on characteristics such as species, breed, age, and location.
- Allow registered users to contact pet owners through the platform.
- Provide a responsive and easy-to-use interface for desktop and mobile users.

## Proposed Solution

PetConnect will be developed as a full-stack web application. Users will be able to register for an account, create pet listings, upload pet photos, and manage their own posts.

Visitors can browse available pets without logging in, while registered users can contact pet owners, save favorite listings, and manage their profiles.

The application will organize information in a structured database, making it easier to search, filter, and maintain pet listings compared to traditional social media posts.

## Features

The initial version of PetConnect will include the following features:

- User registration and login
- User profile management
- Create, edit, and delete pet listings
- Upload pet images
- Search and filter pets
- View detailed pet information
- Contact pet owners through the application
- Favorite pet listings
- Responsive web interface

Future enhancements may include:

- Shelter organization accounts
- Adoption application forms
- AI-powered pet recommendations
- Email notifications
- Location-based search using maps
